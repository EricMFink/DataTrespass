---
title: Kansas Farm Animal and Field Crop and Research Facilities Protection Act

---

The Kansas Farm Animal and Field Crop and Research Facilities Protection Act[^Kansas1] restricts photography and video recording at an "animal facility".[^Kansas2] 





[^Kansas1]: @KansasAgGag2006 [].

[^Kansas2]: @KansasAgGag2006 [sec. c].

    > No person shall, without the effective consent of the owner and with the intent to damage the enteerprise conducted at the animal facility: 
    
    > ... 
	
    > (4\) enter an animal facility to take pictures by photography, video camera or by any other means.
